Rahim has 500 tk. He went to market and purchased 5 eggs, 2 kitkat. Price of each eggs 15tk and each kitkat 80 tk. How much have remaining Rahim's purse ?

Total Amount = 500 Taka.


Eggs Value = 15*5 Taka.

Total = 75 Taka.

====================================

kitkat Value = 80*2 Taka.

Total = 160 Taka.

====================================

Eggs Value Total = 75 Taka.

Kitkat Value Total = 160 Taka.

Overall Total Cost Value = 235 Taka.

====================================

Total Amount= 500 Taka.

Cost Total = 235 Taka.

Remaining = 265 Taka.
